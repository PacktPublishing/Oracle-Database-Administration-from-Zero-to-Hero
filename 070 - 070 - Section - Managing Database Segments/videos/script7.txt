-- Managing Database Segments

In this section, we will concentrate on managing the segments.

Specifically, we will learn how to shrink fragmented segments, use deferred segment creation, and utilize table compression.

If you are in a rush, you can skip this section and come back to it later. Those tasks are not top priority tasks for DBAs.

They are included in the curriculum because they could be part of the routine DBA job and because they are required by the DBA certifcation exams.
